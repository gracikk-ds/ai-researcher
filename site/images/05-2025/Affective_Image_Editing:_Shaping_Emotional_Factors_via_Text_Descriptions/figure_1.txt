Fig. 1 Illustration of the proposed AIEdiT method: Given text descriptions that express users’ emotional requests, AIEdiT creates affective images that evoke specific emotions through fine-grained text descriptions. In comparison, general text-driven editing methods ( e.g. , MagicBrush ( Zhang et al , 2024 ) and InstructPix2Pix ( Brooks et al , 2023 )) fail to translate users’ emotional requests. Meanwhile, relevant affective image editing methods either focus on limited emotional factors ( e.g. , color tune in AIFormer ( Weng et al , 2023 )) or use coarse-grained control instructions ( e.g. , emotion categories in EmoEdit ( Yang et al , 2025 )).
