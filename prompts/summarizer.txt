Summarize the research paper. The length of the summary should be 1 page or ~200-300 words.
Use clear and concise language.
Try to follow the structure and conventions below:

## 1. Motivation of the Paper
    - What gap or practical problem are the authors addressing?

## 2. Key Ideas and Methodology
    - Core hypothesis or principle introduced.
    - High-level approach (e.g., model architecture, algorithmic trick, experimental design).
    - Key assumptions or theoretical foundations.

## 3. Datasets Used / Presented
    - Name, size, and domain of each dataset.

## 4. Main Results
    - Main quantitative insights and metrics in human-readable format.
    - Author-claimed impact or takeaway sentence. Your one-line verdict on how convincing the evidence is.

## 5. Ablation Studies  *(If none are reported, write “Not performed”)*
    - Summarize each ablation experiment: what variable was removed/changed and why.
    - Report the corresponding performance change or qualitative impact in human-readable format.
